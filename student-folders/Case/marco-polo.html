Marco Polo

Marco Polo was a well-known Venetian explorer that was born in Venice, Italy on September 15th, 1254. During the earlier part of his life, he was raised by an aunt an uncle due to the death of his mother. It wasn’t until 1271, when his father and brother returned to meet him, that his travels began.

Marco traveled along the Silk Road between the years of 1271 and 1295, and recorded his travels in multiple books titled, “Marco Polo: The Description of the World”, and “The Travels of Marco Polo”. In these texts, he describes an abundance of location, some of which it is unclear whether or not he actually traveled to these places or based his descriptions off of another person’s account.
